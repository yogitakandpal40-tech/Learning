Unit 1 - Consumer Behaviour

Consumer behaviour studies how people make decisions to buy products. It includes needs, wants, perception, and buying behavior.

Unit 2 - Marketing Research

Marketing research is the process of collecting and analyzing data to understand customer needs and market trends.

Unit 3 - Target Marketing

Target marketing means selecting a specific group of customers and creating strategies to satisfy their needs.
